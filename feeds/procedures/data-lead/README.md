# 📊 Data & Analytics Lead Playbook

A practical, step-by-step playbook for someone stepping into a **Data / Analytics Lead role for the first time, in a new company/workspace.** It covers both halves of the job:

1. **Onboarding** — what *you* do in your first 90 days to land well.
2. **Process** — the data documents and procedures you *produce* for the org.

> Built on one principle: **listen before you change.** Spend your first month earning trust and understanding the data, then improve it deliberately. The whole mandate in one line — make data **trustworthy** AND **used**: the numbers are right, *and* people act on them.

Each procedure follows the house format: a **Mermaid swimlane diagram**, a portable **ASCII flow**, and a **step-by-step responsibility table** — so it works in GitHub, Confluence, or a terminal.

## 🧭 How to Use This Playbook

```
You just got the Data / Analytics Lead role
        │
        ▼
  Read 01 (First 90 Days) ── fill the 30/60/90 template
        │
        ▼
  Days 1–14:  LISTEN  → 1-on-1 template, trace one number end-to-end
  Days 15–30: ASSESS  → 02 + Data Assessment template
  Days 31–60: PLAN    → 03 (Quality & Governance) + 04 (SSOT)
  Days 61–90: EXECUTE → reconcile a metric, 05 + 06 + cadence/metrics
```

> **New to the role and short on time?** Read **01** today, skim **02**, and start your 1-on-1s tomorrow. Then go reconcile the one number leadership distrusts most — it's your fastest trust win. Everything else can wait until you understand the data.
