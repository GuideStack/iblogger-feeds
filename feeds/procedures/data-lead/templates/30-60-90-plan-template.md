**Plan for:** Pich, Data / Analytics Lead

**Mandate:** "Make our numbers trustworthy — leadership has stopped believing the dashboards because every team reports different revenue."

**30 days:** 1-on-1 with all 5 data folks (3 analysts, 2 DEs); mapped 6 key consumers (Finance, Sales, CEO, 3 PMs); traced "revenue" end-to-end and found three live definitions disagreeing by ~8%; data assessment shared with manager by day 28 (headline: SSOT scored 2/5, freshness 2/5 — exec dashboard runs 36h stale, no certified metrics, analysts ~80% reactive tickets).

**60 days:** drafted trust + SSOT plan; top-3 = (1) reconcile & certify revenue, (2) freshness monitoring on exec tables, (3) intake front door to break the ticket queue; proposed a governed metric layer; capacity agreed with manager.

**90 days:** reconciled revenue with Finance + Sales in the room, certified one definition, re-pointed all 4 dashboards (first time the CEO and CFO saw the same number in a year); freshness alerts live; intake form + weekly metric review running; data-health metrics = freshness vs SLA, incident rate, time-to-answer, certified-metric count, self-serve adoption.

**Need:** the CFO's and Sales lead's time for the revenue reconciliation; authority to deprecate the old conflicting dashboards.
