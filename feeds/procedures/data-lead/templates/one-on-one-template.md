## ─── FIRST-MEETING DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (new data lead) ────────────

> Ask these of every analyst & data engineer in your first two weeks. Same questions for each — patterns across answers reveal the real problems (and which numbers nobody trusts).

- What's working well that I should **not** change?
- What's the most frustrating part of your week — technical and otherwise?
- Which dashboard or metric do you **secretly not trust**, and why?
- If you were in my seat, what's the first thing you'd fix?
- How much of your week is reactive tickets vs proactive work?
- What do you want to learn or grow toward this year?
- How do you like to receive feedback?
- What do you wish the previous lead had done differently?
