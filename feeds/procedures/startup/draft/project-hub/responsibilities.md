## Simplified Startup Structure (Early Stage)

In a small startup, the areas above usually combine into **6 core departments**:

1. Leadership & Strategy
2. Product & Technology
3. Operations
4. Marketing & Growth
5. Finance & Compliance
6. Partnerships & External Relations

This is generally enough structure for an early-stage e-commerce company.
