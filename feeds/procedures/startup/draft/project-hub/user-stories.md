# [Platform] — User Stories

**Project:** [Platform] (e-commerce marketplace)
**Document type:** Product requirements — user stories
**Status:** Draft

> Organized by actor: **Buyer**, **Seller**, and **Administrator**.

---

## Buyer

### Account & Registration
- Register via email to create an account
- Set a password
- Verify via OTP
- Provide: name, date of birth, phone number
- Provide a delivery address (e.g., *Toul Kork, Phnom Penh*)
- Accept Terms & Conditions

### Product Discovery
- Browse products by category
- Search for products by name
- View detailed product information before purchasing
- View seller information (know who I am buying from)
- View ratings and reviews

### Shopping Experience
- Add products to cart
- Modify quantities in the cart
- Remove products from the cart
- Review the order before checkout

### Checkout & Payment
- Place an order
- Choose a payment method
- Receive confirmation after placing an order
- View order history

### Order Tracking
- Track order status
- Contact the seller if there is an issue

### Reviews
- Rate products after receiving them
- Leave reviews to help other buyers

---

## Seller

### Registration & Verification
- Register as a vendor
- Submit business information for approval
- Verify identity if required
- Upload a photo for verification purposes
- Provide: store location, phone number, bank account number
- Accept Terms & Conditions

### Store Management
- Create a store profile (store name, product type)
- Upload store logo and description
- Update store information
- Create multiple stores (optional)

### Product Management
- Add new products
- Upload product images
- Set prices
- Update product information
- Remove products that are no longer available
- Manage inventory

### Order Management
- Receive notifications when new orders arrive
- Accept or reject orders
- Update order status (upload receipt after sending the package)
- View full order history

### Finance
- View earnings
- Receive weekly withdrawals (payouts)
- View withdrawal history
- View transaction records

---

## Administrator ([Platform] Team)

### User Management
- Approve seller registrations
- Suspend fraudulent accounts
- Manage buyer and seller accounts

### Marketplace Management
- Manage product categories
- Review reported products
- Remove inappropriate content

### Order Oversight
- View all marketplace orders

### Financial Management
- Track platform commissions
- Track cash flow, income, and transaction activity
- Generate financial reports

### Analytics
- View total sales
- Track active sellers
- Monitor marketplace growth
