# [Platform] — Business Model Canvas

**Project:** [Platform] (e-commerce marketplace)
**Document type:** Strategy — Business Model Canvas
**Status:** 🚧 Draft (to be validated)

> A one-page view of how [Platform] creates, delivers, and captures value. This is a *working hypothesis* — each block should be tested and updated as the team learns. Based on the team's notes: a multi-vendor e-commerce marketplace connecting local sellers (incl. women-led / community vendors via the [Partner Org] network) with buyers.

---

## 1. Customer Segments (Who we serve)
- **Buyers** — local online shoppers (initial market: Phnom Penh / Cambodia) seeking trusted local products.
- **Sellers / Vendors** — small businesses, farms, and producers (e.g. cashew farms, food/seasoning makers, community enterprises) who lack their own online storefront.
- **Partner organizations** — NGOs / programs (e.g. [Partner Org]) that bring vetted vendor networks.

> *Two-sided marketplace: we must grow buyers and sellers together.*

## 2. Value Proposition (Why they choose us)
**For sellers:**
- A ready-made online storefront without building their own site
- Access to a buyer base + marketing reach
- Simple onboarding, payments, and weekly payouts

**For buyers:**
- Discover trusted local products in one place
- See verified seller info, ratings & reviews before buying
- Reliable order tracking and a safe checkout

## 3. Channels (How we reach them)
- The [Platform] web app (primary)
- Social media & community engagement (seller stories, product features)
- Partner-org referrals ([Partner Org] vendor network)
- Word of mouth / seller-driven acquisition (sellers bring their own customers)

## 4. Customer Relationships (How we retain them)
- Self-service registration (email + OTP) and onboarding education
- Customer support & complaint resolution
- Trust & safety (seller verification / KYC, reviews, dispute handling)
- Loyalty over time (reliable delivery, repeat-purchase experience)

## 5. Revenue Streams (How we make money)
- **Commission** on each sale (primary) — % of transaction value
- *(Potential later):* listing/subscription fees for sellers, promoted listings / ads, premium store features, delivery/logistics margin
- **To decide:** commission rate, who bears payment-processing fees

## 6. Key Resources (What we need)
- The platform (engineering team + codebase)
- Vendor relationships & the onboarding pipeline
- Payment & payout infrastructure
- Brand & community trust
- The founding team (see [roles.md](./roles.md))

## 7. Key Activities (What we do)
- Build & maintain the marketplace platform
- Onboard and support vendors
- Acquire buyers & run marketing
- Operate orders, payments, payouts, and trust/safety
- Ensure legal & data-privacy compliance

## 8. Key Partners (Who helps us)
- **[Partner Org]** — vendor sourcing / community network
- Vendors / sellers (supply side)
- Payment provider(s) & banks (payouts)
- Logistics / delivery partners
- Legal & tax advisors

## 9. Cost Structure (What we spend on)
- Platform development & hosting/infrastructure
- Payment processing fees
- Marketing & customer acquisition
- Operations & support staff
- Legal, registration, and compliance costs

---

## ⚠️ Key assumptions to validate
- Sellers will pay a commission in exchange for reach (vs. building their own page).
- Buyers will trust a new marketplace enough to pay online.
- The [Partner Org] network gives a real vendor supply advantage.
- Commission revenue alone can cover costs at early scale.
