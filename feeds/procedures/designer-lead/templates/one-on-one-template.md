## ─── FIRST-MEETING DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (new design lead) ───────────

> Ask these of every designer in your first two weeks. Same questions for each — patterns across answers reveal the real problems.

- What's working well that I should **not** change?
- What's the most frustrating part of your week — craft, process, or people?
- If you were in my seat, what's the first thing you'd fix about how design works here?
- How do you like to receive critique and feedback?
- Where do you feel design gets overruled or treated as decoration?
- What do you want to learn or grow toward this year — deeper craft, research, systems, leading?
- What do you wish the previous design lead had done differently?
