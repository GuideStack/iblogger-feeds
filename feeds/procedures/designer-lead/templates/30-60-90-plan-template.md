# Template: 30/60/90 Day Plan (New Designer Lead)

> **How to use:** Fill this out in your first week and review it with your manager. It turns the [First 90 Days procedure](../01-first-90-days.md) into a concrete, shareable commitment. Keep it to one page. Update it at each 30-day mark. The goal isn't to predict everything — it's to align expectations and show deliberate progress.

# 30/60/90 Plan — Lakena, Designer Lead

**Start date:** 2026-06-01
**Manager:** Vichea (Head of Product)
**Mandate:** "Make the product feel like one product, and stop design being a pixel-ticket service desk."

## 🎯 First 30 Days — LISTEN & ASSESS
| Goal | Measure |
|:-----|:--------|
| 1-on-1 with all 3 designers | 3/3 done |
| Meet PM, PO, 2 Eng leads, QA lead | 5 partners met |
| Use the product + audit live screens | Inconsistency inventory shared (found 6 button styles, 3 date pickers) |
| Design maturity assessment | Shared with Vichea by day 28 |

## 🗺️ Days 31–60 — PLAN
| Goal | Measure |
|:-----|:--------|
| System & ops direction (tokens + top components first) | Reviewed by Eng lead + designers |
| Top 3: no token foundation, broken handoff, zero usability research | Roadmap agreed |
| Buy-in for a standing research slot | One round of 5 users / sprint approved |

## 🚀 Days 61–90 — EXECUTE
| Goal | Measure |
|:-----|:--------|
| Quick win: color + spacing token foundation in design & code | Adopted on new work |
| Quick win: one canonical button (replacing 6) | Button variants 6 → 1 |
| Cadence | Weekly critique + bi-weekly 1-on-1s + design-dev sync live |
| Metrics | System adoption %, one-off count, handoff rework rate, top-task success |

## 🆘 What I need
- One round of 5-user usability tests per sprint (recruiting support from Support team)
- Eng time to implement shared tokens in code
- A standing seat in product discovery, not just delivery handoff
