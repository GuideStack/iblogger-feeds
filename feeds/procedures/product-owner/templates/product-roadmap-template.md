# Template: Product Roadmap (Outcome-Based)

> **How to use:** Build this in Phase 3 once you have a vision and quarterly outcomes. Use **Now / Next / Later** so confidence decreases left to right — *Now* is committed, *Later* is a hypothesis. Every item is a **theme tied to an outcome and a success metric**, never a dated feature list. Revisit each quarter. See [03 — Vision & Roadmap](../03-vision-and-roadmap.md).

# Product Roadmap — Riel Wallet

**Vision:** *For first-time digital-money users in Cambodia, Riel Wallet is the simplest way to send money to anyone with just a phone number.*
**This quarter's North-Star outcome:** First-payment-in-24h: **34% → 60%.**
**Last reviewed:** 2026-07-15 · **Owner:** Sreypov (PO)

| Horizon | Theme | Outcome we want | Success metric | Confidence |
|:--------|:------|:----------------|:---------------|:----------:|
| **NOW** | Fast, fearless first payment | New users complete a transfer fast, without confusion | First-payment-in-24h **34% → 60%** | Committed |
| **NOW** | Frictionless top-up | Users can fund the wallet in under 60s | Top-up completion **+25%** | Committed |
| **NEXT** | Trust & safety | Users feel safe; fewer fraud disputes | Fraud disputes **−30%** | Directional |
| **NEXT** | Bill pay | Recurring reasons to return weekly | Week-4 retention **22% → 35%** | Directional |
| **LATER** | Merchant payments | Explore demand for paying shops | Demand / fit — TBD | Hypothesis |

**Explicitly NOT now:** *Merchant payments* — Sales is pushing hard for it, but it doesn't move activation, this quarter's North-Star. It stays in *Later* until first-payment activation clears 60%. (Bopha is backing this call.)

---

### Notes on the example
- Every row names a **theme + outcome + metric** — there is no "build the onboarding wizard" feature row, because *how* to hit 60% is the team's to design in refinement.
- The **NOT now** line is doing real work: it's the documented "no" to Sales (see [05 — saying no](../05-prioritization-and-value.md#saying-no--managing-trade-offs)).
- *Later* is labeled a **hypothesis** so no stakeholder mistakes it for a commitment.
