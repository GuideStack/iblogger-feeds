# Template: 30/60/90 Day Plan (New Product Owner)

> **How to use:** Fill this out in your first week and review it with your sponsor / Business Owner. It turns the [First 90 Days procedure](../01-first-90-days.md) into a concrete, shareable commitment. Keep it to one page. Update it at each 30-day mark. Remember the discipline: **don't re-order the backlog in the first 30 days — listen and assess first.**

# 30/60/90 Plan — Sreypov, Product Owner

**Start date:** 2026-06-01
**Sponsor / Business Owner:** Bopha (VP Product)
**Product:** "Riel Wallet" — consumer mobile wallet (send/receive money, top-up, bill pay)
**Mandate:** "Get new users to actually use the wallet — activation is flat and the backlog is a 300-item graveyard."
**Decision authority:** Confirmed with Bopha — Sreypov has final say on backlog order and can decline stakeholder requests with a reason; Bopha backs the "no."

**30 days:** 1-on-1 with all 6 team members + SM + delivery PM; read 3 months of support tickets (top complaint: "first transfer is confusing"); watched analytics — only **34%** of new users complete a first payment in 24h. Scored the 6 dimensions: **Vision 1, Backlog health 1, Story quality 2, Prioritization 2, Stakeholder alignment 2, Value measurement 1.** Assessment shared with Bopha day 28. Headline: *there is no vision, no single owner of order, and 300 items nobody trusts.*

**60 days:** Co-wrote the vision with Bopha — *"For first-time digital-money users in Cambodia, Riel Wallet is the simplest way to send money to anyone with just a phone number."* Set quarterly outcome: **first-payment-in-24h from 34% → 60%.** Archived 210 dead backlog items (down from 300 to 90); published ONE ordered list. Top theme: *fast, fearless first payment.*

**90 days:** Ran refinement weekly — top 2 sprints always Ready with Given/When/Then AC. Accepted/rejected at review against AC (rejected the "send by phone number" story once — AC #4 on invalid numbers wasn't met). Activation metric live on a dashboard; first cohort moved **34% → 47%** by day 90.

**Need from sponsor:** continued air-cover when Sales pushes the "merchant payments" feature — it's *Later*, not *Now*, and I need Bopha to hold that line with them.
