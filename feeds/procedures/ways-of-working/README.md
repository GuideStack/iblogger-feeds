# 🤝 Ways of Working

Practices and mindsets that make a *system* or *procedure* actually work — and let a team collaborate around it. Less about a specific tool or role, more about the habits of learning and improvement that hold everything else together.

> Frameworks here often come written up from talks, books, and operators — the recurring theme: a process is only as good as the **loop** that keeps improving it and the **mindset** of the people running it.
