# 💼 Business Owner Leadership Playbook

A practical, step-by-step playbook for someone stepping into a **Business Owner / Product-line Owner role for the first time, in a new company/workspace** — the executive **sponsor** accountable for a product or business line's P&L, vision, and ROI. (Strategy & outcomes focus. This is *not* the Agile Product Owner, and *not* a startup founder — it's the person a PM/PO reports up to.) It covers both halves of the job:

1. **Onboarding** — what *you* do in your first 90 days to land well.
2. **Strategy craft** — the vision, OKRs, budget, governance, and metrics you *own* for the business line.

> Built on one principle: **own the outcome, empower the doers.** You set the WHY (vision, strategy) and the WHAT-MATTERS (success metrics, priorities) and you fund it — the PO/PM/EM and their teams figure out the WHAT and the HOW. Spend your first month understanding the business before you steer it.

Each procedure follows the house format: a **Mermaid swimlane diagram**, a portable **ASCII flow**, and a **step-by-step responsibility table** — so it works in GitHub, Confluence, or a terminal.

## 🧭 How to Use This Playbook

```
You just became the Business Owner / Sponsor
        │
        ▼
  Read 01 (First 90 Days) ── fill the 30/60/90 template
        │
        ▼
  Days 1–14:  LISTEN  → meet exec, PM/PO/EM, customers; read the numbers
  Days 15–30: ASSESS  → 02 + Business & Product Assessment
  Days 31–60: PLAN    → 03 (Vision/OKRs) + 04 (Budget/ROI) + north-star
  Days 61–90: EXECUTE → 05, 06 + funded bets + KPI dashboard
```

> **New to the role and short on time?** Read **01** today, skim **02**, and start meeting your delivery leads and customers tomorrow. Everything else can wait until you understand the business.
