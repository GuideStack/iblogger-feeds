# North-Star & KPIs — BillFlow

**Owner:** Dara Chea · **Date:** 2026-05-31

## 🌟 North-Star
| North-star | Current | Target (Q4 2026) | Why this one |
|:-----------|:--------|:-----------------|:-------------|
| Weekly active paying accounts | 2,100 | 2,600 | Captures real, recurring value — an account using billing weekly is getting value and will renew. Beats "total signups," which we were vanity-tracking before. |

## 🔢 Supporting Input Metrics
| Input metric | Current | Target | Drives the north-star by… |
|:-------------|:--------|:-------|:--------------------------|
| New-account activation (≤14d) | 34% | 50% | more accounts reach value → fewer churn early |
| Net revenue retention | 96% | 100% | keeps & expands the active base |
| Trial → paid conversion | 19% | 26% | grows the top of the active base |

## 📊 Business KPIs
| KPI | Type | Current | Target | Reads the… |
|:----|:-----|:--------|:-------|:-----------|
| Activation rate | 🟢 Leading | 34% | 50% | future (predicts retention) |
| Trial→paid | 🟢 Leading | 19% | 26% | future |
| ARR | 🔵 Lagging | $4.2M | $4.8M | result |
| Gross margin | 🔵 Lagging | 71% | ≥71% | result |
| Logo churn | 🔵 Lagging | 11%/yr | ≤8%/yr | result |

## 🛡️ Guardrails
| Guardrail | Floor / ceiling | Why |
|:----------|:----------------|:----|
| NPS | ≥ 42 | don't chase activation by harassing users |
| Support tickets / new account | ≤ +10% | growth shouldn't overload the team |
| Monthly burn | within budget | protect runway while investing |

> All metrics are team- and product-level outcomes. BillFlow does **not** track individual activity (commits, hours, tickets-per-person) — that would surveil people and optimize the wrong thing.
