# Template: OKR (Objective & Key Results)

> **How to use:** Use this to set quarterly direction, following the [Vision, Strategy & OKRs procedure](../03-vision-strategy-and-okrs.md). As Business Owner you set the **Objective** (the outcome); the teams set their own **Key Results** (the measurable signals) so they own them. Keep it to 2–4 objectives total, 3–5 key results each. Objectives are outcomes — never a feature list.

# OKRs — BillFlow, Q3 2026

**Owner:** Dara Chea · **North-star:** weekly active paying accounts

---

## Objective: Make BillFlow effortless for a new account to get value fast
> Set by: Dara (BO). The PO and team authored the key results below.

| # | Key Result | Baseline | Target | Owner (team) |
|:--|:-----------|:---------|:-------|:-------------|
| KR1 | New-account activation (first invoice ≤14d) | 34% | 50% | Onboarding squad |
| KR2 | Net revenue retention | 96% | 100% | Retention squad |
| KR3 | Weekly active paying accounts | 2,100 | 2,450 | Whole line |

**Cascading note:** Dara set the objective ("effortless first value") and the north-star link. The onboarding and retention squads picked their own KRs and targets — so they're commitments, not handed-down quotas.

**Guardrail:** support ticket volume per new account must not rise more than 10%, and NPS must stay ≥ 42 (no growth-by-spamming).

> Note: BillFlow does *not* tie anyone's pay to OKR hit-rate — that would just buy sandbagged, safe numbers.
