## ─── FIRST-MEETING DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (new lead) ─────────────────

> Ask these of every agent and CSM in your first two weeks. Same questions for each — patterns across answers reveal the real problems.

- What's working well that I should **not** change?
- What's the most frustrating part of your week?
- Which tickets or accounts keep you up at night?
- If you were in my seat, what's the first thing you'd fix?
- How do you like to receive feedback?
- What do you want to learn or grow toward this year?
- Do you feel you can take real breaks / time off? *(burnout check)*
- What do you wish the previous lead had done differently?
