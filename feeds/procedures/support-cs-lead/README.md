# 🎧 Support & Customer Success Lead Playbook

A practical, step-by-step playbook for someone stepping into a **Support / Customer Success Lead role for the first time, in a new company/workspace.** It covers both halves of the job:

1. **Onboarding** — what *you* do in your first 90 days to land well.
2. **Operations** — the support and customer-success documents and procedures you *produce* for the team.

> Built on one principle: **listen before you change.** Spend your first month earning trust and understanding the customer and the team, then improve the system deliberately. The core shift: from top agent/CSM to a **multiplier accountable for customer outcomes** — and the **voice of the customer** inside the company.

This role spans two related but distinct disciplines: **Support is reactive** (resolve issues, tickets, SLAs) and **Customer Success is proactive** (drive adoption, retention, expansion, reduce churn). In smaller orgs one lead owns both — this playbook covers leading a team that does both, and notes throughout where Support vs CS practices differ.

Each procedure follows the house format: a **Mermaid swimlane diagram**, a portable **ASCII flow**, and a **step-by-step responsibility table** — so it works in GitHub, Confluence, or a terminal.

## 🧭 How to Use This Playbook

```
You just got the Support / CS Lead role
        │
        ▼
  Read 01 (First 90 Days) ── fill the 30/60/90 template
        │
        ▼
  Days 1–14:  LISTEN  → 1-on-1 template, discovery questions, shadow the queue
  Days 15–30: ASSESS  → 02 + Support & CS Assessment template
  Days 31–60: PLAN    → 03 (SLAs) + 04 (escalation) + SLA policy template
  Days 61–90: EXECUTE → 05 (retention) + 06 (knowledge/team) + metrics
```

> **New to the role and short on time?** Read **01** today, skim **02**, and start your 1-on-1s tomorrow. Everything else can wait until you understand the customers and the team.
