# 🧪 QA Leadership Playbook

A practical, step-by-step playbook for someone stepping into a **QA Lead role for the first time, in a new company/workspace.** It covers both halves of the job:

1. **Onboarding** — what *you* do in your first 90 days to land well.
2. **Process** — the QA documents and procedures you *produce* for the team.

> Built on one principle: **listen before you change.** Spend your first month earning trust and understanding the system, then improve it deliberately.

Each procedure follows the house format: a **Mermaid swimlane diagram**, a portable **ASCII flow**, and a **step-by-step responsibility table** — so it works in GitHub, Confluence, or a terminal.

## 🧭 How to Use This Playbook

```
You just got the QA Lead role
        │
        ▼
  Read 01 (First 90 Days) ── fill the 30/60/90 template
        │
        ▼
  Days 1–14:  LISTEN  → 1-on-1 template, discovery questions
  Days 15–30: ASSESS  → 02 + Assessment Report template
  Days 31–60: PLAN    → 03 (Test Strategy)
  Days 61–90: EXECUTE → 04, 05, 06 + cadence/metrics
```

> **New to the role and short on time?** Read **01** today, skim **02**, and start your 1-on-1s tomorrow. Everything else can wait until you understand the team.
